Download ConvoyMP

Installation is quick and easy. Follow the steps below to get started.

System Requirements

Minimum

  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit
  • CPU: Intel Core i5-4590 / AMD FX-8350
  • RAM: 8 GB
  • GPU: NVIDIA GTX 760 / AMD Radeon R9 280
  • Storage: 5 GB free space
  • Network: Broadband internet connection

Recommended

  • OS: Windows 11 64-bit
  • CPU: Intel Core i7-10700 / AMD Ryzen 7 3700X
  • RAM: 16 GB
  • GPU: NVIDIA RTX 3060 / AMD RX 6600 XT
  • Storage: 5 GB SSD
  • Network: Broadband internet connection

Also Requires

  • Euro Truck Simulator 2 - Steam version (latest update)
  • DirectX: Version 11 or higher
  • .NET Framework: 4.7.2 or higher

Installation Steps

  1. Register an Account - Create a free ConvoyMP account on our website.
  2. Download the Launcher - Click the download button below.
  3. Connect Steam - Link your Steam account that owns ETS2.
  4. Install & Play - Run the installer, launch ConvoyMP, and start trucking!

Rules

Follow these rules to keep ConvoyMP fun for everyone.

🚗 Simulation Rules

  1. Drive on the correct side - Always drive on the right-hand side of the road (except in the UK).
  2. Use your indicators - Signal before turning, changing lanes, or merging.
  3. Obey traffic lights and signs - Stop at red lights, yield at junctions, respect speed limits.
  4. No speeding - Follow posted speed limits. Driving too fast is dangerous in traffic.
  5. Use your mirrors - Check mirrors before overtaking or changing lanes.
  6. Park properly - Do not park on roads, at intersections, or in fuel station forecourts.
  7. No reckless driving - Drive smoothly. No swerving, weaving, or erratic behaviour.
  8. Follow motorway rules - Use the correct lane. Overtake on the left is not allowed.
  9. Headlights at night - Use headlights between 22:00 and 06:00 in-game time.

🎮 In-Game Rules

  1. No blocking roads - Do not block roads, intersections, roundabouts, or weigh stations on purpose.
  2. No griefing - Do not intentionally ram, crash into, or push other players.
  3. No cutting off - Do not pull in front of trucks and brake suddenly.
  4. No backwards driving - Never drive against the flow of traffic.
  5. No ramming - Do not use your truck as a weapon. Accidents happen, but deliberate ramming is a ban.
  6. No exploiting - Do not abuse game bugs or glitches to gain an advantage.
  7. No AFK on roads - Do not leave your truck idle on a road or in a lane.
  8. Convoy rules - When in a convoy, follow the leader and maintain spacing. Do not overtake unless instructed.
  9. Trailer rules - Do not disconnect your trailer on public roads or use it to block others.

🌐 Website & Chat Rules

  1. Be respectful - Treat everyone with respect. No harassment, bullying, or personal attacks.
  2. No hate speech - Racist, sexist, homophobic, or any discriminatory language is a permanent ban.
  3. No spamming - Do not spam messages, emojis, images, or links in community chat.
  4. No offensive content - No NSFW, gore, or inappropriate images, videos, or links.
  5. No advertising - Do not promote other servers, Discord servers, or services without permission.
  6. English in main chat - Keep community chat in English so moderators can moderate.
  7. No impersonation - Do not pretend to be a staff member or another player.
  8. Protect your account - Do not share your login details. You are responsible for your account.
  9. No alt accounts - Do not create multiple accounts to avoid bans or restrictions.
  10. Follow staff instructions - Moderators and admins have the final say in all matters.

🔎 Report System Rules

  1. Only report real incidents - Do not file false reports or reports as a joke.
  2. Provide evidence - Screenshots or video clips greatly help your report.
  3. Be specific - Include the player's name, server, date/time, and a detailed description.
  4. No report trading - Do not offer to remove a report in exchange for anything.
  5. Report the correct rule - Select the rule that was actually broken.
  6. Be patient - Reports are reviewed within 48 hours. Do not spam or duplicate reports.
  7. No报复 reporting - Do not file a report just because someone reported you.
  8. Appeals - If you are banned, you have the right to appeal. Use the ban appeal system, not report spam.

Breaking these rules may result in a warning, temporary ban, or permanent ban from ConvoyMP. Our AI moderation system automatically filters inappropriate language in chat. Serious offences (hate speech, hacking, deliberate ramming) result in immediate bans.

Guides & Support

Need help? Check our guides or contact the support team.

How do I register?

Click the Register button on the top right of the website, fill in your details, and you will receive a ConvoyMP ID (e.g. CMP-A3F7K). Your first account becomes the admin automatically.

What is my ConvoyMP ID?

Every player gets a unique ConvoyMP ID when they register. It looks like CMP-A3F7K. You can find it on your Account Dashboard. Other players can use it to search for you.

How do I find other players?

Go to the Players page in the navbar and search by username or ConvoyMP ID. Click on a player card to see their profile, distance driven, and jobs completed.

ConvoyMP won't launch

Make sure ETS2 is updated on Steam. Run the launcher as administrator. Check that you meet the system requirements on the Download page.

How do I link my Steam account?

Go to Account Settings after logging in and click "Link Steam Account". This is required to play on ConvoyMP servers.

How do I upload a profile picture?

Go to your Account Dashboard and click "Upload Image" under the Profile Picture section. You can upload any image up to 5MB.

How do I report a player?

Go to Account > Report Player in the sidebar. Fill in the offender's username, the server, which rule was broken, and describe what happened. Our team reviews reports within 48 hours.

How do I appeal a ban?

Go to Account > Ban Appeals. Explain why you believe the ban was unfair. Our team will review your appeal and respond.

Step 1: Register an Account

Create a free ConvoyMP account on this website. You will receive a unique ConvoyMP Player ID.

Step 2: Download the Launcher

Go to the Download page and click "Download ConvoyMP Launcher". Save the installer to your computer.

Step 3: Install the Launcher

Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions. Choose your ETS2 installation directory when prompted.

Step 4: Link Your Steam Account

Open the ConvoyMP launcher and log in with your ConvoyMP credentials. Click "Link Steam" and authorise access to your Steam account that owns ETS2.

Step 5: Play!

Select a server from the server list, click "Connect", and start trucking with other players across Europe!

Setting Up Your Profile

After registering, go to your Account Dashboard to set up your profile:

  • Upload a profile picture so other players can recognise you
  • Add a banner image to personalise your profile
  • Write a bio to tell other truckers about yourself
  • Your ConvoyMP ID is displayed on your dashboard - share it with friends

Joining Your First Server

Go to the Servers page to see which servers are online. Pick a server with available slots and connect through the ConvoyMP launcher. Start in a quiet area to get used to multiplayer driving.

Using Community Chat

Visit the Community page to chat with other truckers in real-time. You can share screenshots, videos, and use emojis. Upload files directly using the upload button.

Finding Other Players

Use the Players page to search for friends or other truckers by username or ConvoyMP ID. Click on a player card to see their profile and stats.

Launcher won't open

Try running as administrator. Make sure .NET Framework 4.7.2 or higher is installed. Check that antivirus isn't blocking the launcher.

Can't connect to server

Check your internet connection. Make sure your firewall allows ConvoyMP through. Try a different server. Check the Servers page to see if the server is online.

Game crashes on launch

Verify ETS2 game files on Steam. Remove any other ETS2 mods that may conflict. Make sure ETS2 is updated to the latest version.

Low FPS in multiplayer

Lower your graphics settings in ETS2. Reduce the draw distance. Close background applications. Make sure your GPU drivers are up to date.

Upload not working

Make sure the file is under 10MB. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, MP4, MP3. Check your internet connection. Try refreshing the page.
